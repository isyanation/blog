<blackquotes>"Turut berduka cita untuk Mas Tukul, semoga beliau diterima di sisi-Nya," tulis Isyana Sarasvati.</blackquotes><br/>

"@isyanasarasvati gw nangkepnya tuh kayak yg meninggal mas Tukul," sahut @justforNaa.
</blackquotes>
